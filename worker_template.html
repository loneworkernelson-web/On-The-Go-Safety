Safety & Duress PINs

Standard Safety PIN: This code is used exclusively to declare yourself safe after an alarm has been activated. Entering this PIN is the only way to confirm to the system that you are secure and to stop the alert from escalating.

Silent Duress PIN: This is used to silently declare yourself unsafe when you are required to clear an alarm under threat. The app will appear to clear the alarm normally, but it will immediately and silently notify your contacts that you require help.
